# ReguFlow AI

## Product Requirements Document

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Product Overview

ReguFlow AI is an AI-powered Regulation-to-Action Compliance Engine designed for banks and financial institutions.

The platform transforms regulatory updates into actionable compliance workflows by:

* Monitoring regulatory sources
* Ingesting compliance documents
* Detecting clause-level changes
* Performing impact analysis
* Generating Measurable Action Points (MAPs)
* Tracking compliance execution
* Improving audit readiness

The core philosophy of ReguFlow AI is:

Regulation → Action → Proof

Unlike traditional compliance tools that stop at alerts or summaries, ReguFlow AI operationalizes compliance execution.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Banks continuously receive regulatory updates from:

* RBI
* SEBI
* NPCI
* CERT-In
* Internal Compliance Teams

Current compliance processes are:

* Manual
* Time-consuming
* Difficult to audit
* Fragmented across departments

Compliance teams spend significant effort:

* Reading regulations
* Identifying changes
* Determining business impact
* Assigning responsibilities
* Tracking implementation
* Preparing audit evidence

This creates:

* Delayed compliance
* Audit risk
* Operational inefficiency
* Regulatory exposure

---

# 3. Product Vision

Create an intelligent compliance operating system capable of:

1. Understanding regulatory changes.
2. Identifying affected business areas.
3. Generating actionable compliance tasks.
4. Tracking implementation.
5. Producing audit-ready evidence.

The platform should reduce compliance response time from days to hours.
