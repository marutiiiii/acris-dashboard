# Prototype Phase Criteria

This hackathon evaluates the final prototype across 4 categories, each carrying 25 marks, for a total of 100 marks.

## 1. Problem Understanding — 25 Marks
**Description:**  
Depth of insight into the problem. Are we solving the right pain point?

**What judges want:**
- Clear understanding of the banking compliance problem
- Evidence that the solution targets a real operational pain point
- Strong explanation of why the problem matters
- Correctly scoped use case for banks and compliance teams

**How ReguFlow AI should score well:**
- Show manual compliance delays, audit risk, and fragmented workflows
- Explain why alert-only tools are insufficient
- Demonstrate regulation-to-action-to-proof as the right pain point

---

## 2. Originality / Innovation — 25 Marks
**Description:**  
Is the idea novel or a significant improvement over existing solutions?

**What judges want:**
- A fresh approach, not a generic dashboard
- Clear differentiators from existing tools
- A solution that feels intelligent and advanced

**How ReguFlow AI should score well:**
- Clause-level change detection
- Measurable Action Points (MAPs)
- Adaptive Compliance Copilot
- Regulation-to-Action-to-Proof workflow
- AI-assisted evidence validation and audit traceability

---

## 3. Technical Implementation — 25 Marks
**Description:**  
How well is it built? Code quality, architecture, functionality.

**What judges want:**
- Clean and working prototype
- Solid UI structure and workflow
- Good frontend architecture
- Realistic interactions and polished execution

**How ReguFlow AI should score well:**
- Fully connected frontend pages
- Smooth navigation and state handling
- Mock workflows that feel real
- Strong UI consistency and enterprise design
- Working demo flows for PDF analysis, MAPs, and audit readiness

---

## 4. Real-World Applicability — 25 Marks
**Description:**  
Is the solution practical, scalable, or impactful in real-world use cases?

**What judges want:**
- Practical banking use case
- Clear business value
- Scalability and operational relevance
- Impact on compliance, audit, and risk management

**How ReguFlow AI should score well:**
- Helps banks manage regulations faster
- Improves audit readiness
- Reduces manual effort and compliance delays
- Supports compliance, legal, IT, cybersecurity, and audit teams

---

## Scoring Strategy
To maximize marks:
- Focus on a real banking pain point
- Show a unique compliance workflow
- Make the prototype look enterprise-ready
- Ensure the demo clearly shows business impact
- Keep all frontend experiences smooth, realistic, and polished
